The feed is optimized to never let you leave. This page is optimized to let you go.

There are no infinite scrolls here, no notifications, no counter ticking up beside a
heart. Just words, posted when they're worth posting, on a page that loads instantly
and asks nothing of you.

I think that's worth protecting. A slow web — one you visit on purpose, the way you'd
visit a friend — is where the good thinking happens. Not the reactions. The thinking.

So this is the deal: I'll write things I actually mean, and you can read them whenever
you like. No urgency. No streak to keep. Just a place that will still be here, exactly
like this, when you come back.
