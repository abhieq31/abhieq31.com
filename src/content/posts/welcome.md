This is my corner of the internet — a place I plan to keep for a long time.

No algorithm decides what shows up here. No feed pushes the next thing in front
of you. Just writing, posted when I have something worth saying, and kept in one
place I control.

I'll use it for essays, short notes, and the occasional recording. Some posts
will be polished. Most won't be. That's the point — to think in public and leave
a trail I can look back on.

If you want the new stuff as it lands, [subscribe](/subscribe) or follow along on
the socials in the header. Otherwise, the [archive](/archive) has everything.

Thanks for being here.
