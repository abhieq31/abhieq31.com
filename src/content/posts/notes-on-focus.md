---
title: Notes on Focus
subtitle: A few things that actually helped
date: 2026-04-08
---

Focus isn't a personality trait. It's a set of conditions you can arrange.

A few that work for me:

- **One open loop at a time.** I write down everything else so my head stops
  holding it.
- **Long blocks, not many small ones.** Depth needs runway.
- **Boredom before phone.** The urge to check passes if I let it.
- **Finish, then judge.** Editing while drafting kills both.

None of this is new. The hard part isn't knowing it — it's doing it on the days
you don't feel like it.
